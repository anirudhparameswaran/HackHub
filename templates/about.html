How to use


This service is used to predict the damage caused by an earthquake depending on the parameters brin input. Details about the parameters are:

City


Select the city you wish to inquire about.


Building Height


Enter the height of the building in feet.


Condition


Condition defines the state of the building at present, with a higher value signifying worse condition.


Floor Count


Number of floors in the building.


Ward ID


This defines the ward ID of the area in which the building is situated at. This information is used by our model to determine the damage the building will take.


Area (Sq.Ft.)


The total area of the building in square feet.


Building Age


Age of the building in years.


VCMUN ID


This is the VCMUN ID which again plays a major role in determining the damage taken by the building.


Area Assesed


The total area assesed.
